## ✨ What it does

Stop digging through endless folders. Lumen indexes your whole pack and makes everything
findable and previewable instantly.

- ⚡ **Instant search** across 90,000+ files — full-text, by type, pack or tag, in milliseconds.
- 🎬 **Rich previews** — videos play, audio shows waveforms, images full-frame, fonts render live,
  3D models orbit, image sequences scrub, text & LUTs preview inline.
- 🏷️ **Tags, notes & collections** — organize across packs your way, tag in bulk, jot notes.
- 🖱️ **Drag straight into your editor** — drop any asset (or a whole multi-selection) into
  Premiere, After Effects, DaVinci or Explorer.
- 🗂️ **Smart library** — folders, favorites, recents, categories, image-sequence grouping,
  font-family grouping, in-app trash with restore.
- 🎛️ **Tuned for any PC** — virtualized grid, background thumbnail warm-up, quality presets.
- 🔄 **Auto-updates** — you always get the latest version automatically.

## ⬇️ Download & install

1. Grab the latest **`Lumen Setup x.x.x.exe`** from the [**Releases**](../../releases/latest) page.
2. Run it. Windows SmartScreen may say *"unknown publisher"* → **More info → Run anyway**
   (the app isn't code-signed yet — that's normal for a new app).
3. Pick an install folder, finish, launch. 🌙

> **Updates are automatic** — when a new version ships, Lumen downloads it in the background and
> applies it on the next restart. No reinstalling.

---

## 🔑 Activation

Lumen is licensed per device.

1. On first launch, enter the **license key** you received (`LUMEN-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX`).
2. The key locks to your computer and unlocks the app.
3. Changed PC? Ask for a **HWID reset** and re-activate on the new machine.

You need to be **online** when launching so your license can be verified.

---

## 🖥️ System requirements

- **Windows 10 or 11** (64-bit)
- ~250 MB free disk space for the app (your asset library lives wherever you keep it)
- An internet connection for activation & updates
